Video Share: When Yogurt Took Over

The short film tells the audience a sci-fi story about the rapid evolution of yogurt from "evolution" to ruling the earth to the universe. What I think is particularly interesting is that it is different from other science fiction films. It describes a new kind of "race" from awakening to breaking free from human control and finally ruling the earth. In fact, it does not need to launch any genocide plan or large-scale war. It only needs to use "normal" political, economic, and technological means.
